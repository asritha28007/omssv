Comfortable Bedroom

Spacious, airy rooms designed for rest and recovery. Each bed has a personal reading lamp.

  • Single cot with thick foam mattress
  • Personal study table & ergonomic chair
  • Dedicated wardrobe per student
  • Power outlets at each bedside
  • AC or Fan as per room type
📚
Quiet Study Room

A dedicated study room with excellent lighting and high-speed WiFi.

  • 100 Mbps fiber WiFi
  • Individual study carrels
  • Whiteboard & stationery
  • AC room – always comfortable
  • Open till 11 PM daily
🚿
Clean Washroom

Hygienic bathrooms cleaned twice daily. Hot water available mornings and evenings.

  • Hot water 6–9 AM & 6–9 PM
  • Cleaned twice daily
  • Western & Indian toilets
  • Good ventilation & lighting
  • Geyser in attached rooms

Common Lounge

A warm common room where residents bond, relax, and celebrate festivals.

  • LED TV with cable
  • Indoor games (chess, carom)
  • Comfortable seating
  • Charging stations
  • Monthly cultural nights

Mess Hall

Spacious dining hall serving 4 wholesome meals daily with a rotating South & North Indian menu.

  • Breakfast 7:30–9:30 AM
